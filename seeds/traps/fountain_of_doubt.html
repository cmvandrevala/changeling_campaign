The Fountain of Doubt

Description

Appearance

The Fountain of Doubt is a beautiful marble fountain that sends a twinkling stream of water into the air. It is covered in a series of seemingly contradictory carvings that depict people having good, neutral, and bad luck:
  • A person drinking from the fountain, about to get struck by lightning
  • A person drinking from the fountain, surrounded by heaps of gold and jewels
  • A person drinking from the fountain, nothing else around
  • A person drinking from the fountain, a tiger about to pounce on him
  • A person drinking from the fountain, he seems to have gotten he power to throw fire

Effects

Drinking from the fountain:

  1. Heals one lethal wound (once per day).
  2. Regains one willpower (once per day).
  3. Gives a -1 dice penalty to all rolls until the next time you pee.
  4. Is a breaking point for clarity 7.
